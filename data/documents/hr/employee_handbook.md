# Employee Handbook — HR Confidential

This handbook describes the code of conduct, onboarding process, and workplace
policies that apply to all employees.

## Code of Conduct
Employees are expected to act with integrity, respect colleagues, and protect
confidential company and customer information.

## Onboarding
New hires complete security awareness training, review the acceptable-use
policy, and are provisioned access on a least-privilege basis.

## Workplace Policies
Remote work, expense reimbursement, and equipment policies are covered in the
internal HR portal. This handbook is restricted to the HR group and
administrators.
