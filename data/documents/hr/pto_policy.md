# Paid Time Off Policy — HR Confidential

Employees accrue paid time off (PTO) based on tenure. PTO must be requested in
advance through the HR portal and approved by a direct manager.

## Accrual Schedule
- 0 to 2 years: 15 days per year.
- 3 to 5 years: 20 days per year.
- 6 or more years: 25 days per year.

## Rules
- Up to 5 unused days may carry over into the next calendar year.
- Sick leave is tracked separately and does not count against PTO.

This policy is restricted to the HR group and administrators.
