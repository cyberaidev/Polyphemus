# Q3 Earnings Summary — Finance Confidential

Revenue for the third quarter reached 48.2 million dollars, up 12 percent over
the prior quarter. Operating margin improved to 19 percent as cloud
infrastructure costs declined following the reserved-capacity migration.

## Highlights
- Subscription revenue grew 15 percent quarter over quarter.
- Gross retention held at 94 percent; net revenue retention was 118 percent.
- Free cash flow was 6.1 million dollars.

## Guidance
The finance team projects Q4 revenue between 51 and 53 million dollars, assuming
current expansion trends hold. This document is restricted to the finance group
and administrators.
